Services

Peace and tranquility is dedicated to the practise of cognitive behavioral therapy as treatment for a variety of psychological problems including issues like anxiety,depression and adjustment to significant life stressors.

psychological Disorders Treated

    Individual therapy

    Relationship counselling

    Anxiety and depression

    Loss and grief

    self esteem and confidence

    work related issues

    Bullying

    Drugs,alcohol and Gambling

    perinatal depression

    Body image

    sexual issues and abuse

    suicidal thoughts

Initial Assessment

First sessions will be devoted to comprehensive diagnostic assesment of presenting problem and symptoms,After assessment information about your diagnosis and initial treatment plans will be provided.If your presenting problem require treatment that cannot be provided appropriate referals will be given to you.

Therapy sessions

Most treatment plans recommend weekly sessions which are 45 to 60minutes in leghth however frequency of sessions and overall duration of treatment varies depending on nature of presenting problems.

Billing and Payment

Payment will be collected in full before each session,credit card,personal check or cash are accepted.each session costs 2500.
